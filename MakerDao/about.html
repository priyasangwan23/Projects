About MakerDAO

MakerDAO is an open-source project on the Ethereum blockchain and a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) created in 2014.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive platform that empowers people across the globe to participate in an open financial system using decentralized technology.

Our Team

MakerDAO’s community includes developers, risk managers, and governance contributors from around the world.
