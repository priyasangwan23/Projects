Dai Stablecoin

Dai is a decentralized stablecoin soft-pegged to the U.S. dollar. Users can generate Dai by locking collateral in the Maker Vaults.

Vaults

Vaults are smart contracts where users deposit collateral (like ETH) to generate Dai, helping to maintain Dai’s stability and backing.
